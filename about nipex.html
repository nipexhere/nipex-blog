About Nipex

AfroEmo Artiste.

connecting with emotions to make the best of music

NIPEX Pioneer of the Afro-Emo sound was born and bred in Lagos, Nigeria.
Here he was exposed to various musical artistes and cultures from a young age which has shaped him musically.
He dropped his first single Dead Love exclusively on audiomack and soundcloud on the 15th October 2021...
It has been a roller coaster ever since.
