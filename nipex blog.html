Nipex

AfroEmo Artiste.

connecting with emotions to make the best of music

Nipex . Pioneer of the Afro-Emo sound was born and bred in Lagos,Nigeria; where he was exposed to various different musicians and artists from a young age which has shaped him musically
He dropped his first single Dead Love exclusively on audiomack and soundcloud on the 15th October 2021... this is also historic as afrobeat legend Fela Kuti was born on the same day
